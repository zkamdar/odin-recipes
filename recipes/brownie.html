Brownie Recipe

Description

Fudgy brownies are a type of dessert that is known for its rich, dense, and moist texture. Unlike cake-like brownies, which are light and airy, fudgy brownies are dense and fudgy, with a texture that is almost like a cross between a cake and a chocolate truffle. They are typically made with melted chocolate, butter, sugar, eggs, and flour, and are often topped with chocolate chips, nuts, or frosting. Fudgy brownies are often considered to be the ultimate indulgence for chocolate lovers, and are often served as a dessert or a snack.

Ingredients

  • Chocolate: You will need melted chocolate for the base of the brownie batter. Use a high-quality chocolate that has a deep, rich flavor. You can use semisweet, bittersweet, or dark chocolate, depending on your preference.
  • Butter: This adds richness and moisture to the brownies. Use unsalted butter so you can control the amount of salt in the recipe.
  • Sugar: This adds sweetness to the brownies and helps to give them their characteristic fudgy texture. Use white granulated sugar for the best results.
  • Eggs: These help to bind the ingredients together and give the brownies their structure. Use large eggs at room temperature.
  • Flour: This provides the structure for the brownies and helps them to hold their shape. Use all-purpose flour for the best results.
  • Salt: This helps to balance the sweetness of the brownies and enhance the flavor of the chocolate. Use a fine sea salt or table salt.
  • In addition to these basic ingredients, you can also add other ingredients to give your brownies extra flavor and texture. Some common additions include chocolate chips, nuts (such as walnuts or pecans), vanilla extract, and cocoa powder. Experiment with different combinations to find your favorite!

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit (175 degrees Celsius) and line a 9x9-inch square baking pan with parchment paper.
  2. In a medium saucepan, melt the chocolate and butter over low heat, stirring constantly, until smooth and melted. Remove from the heat and let cool slightly.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the sugar and eggs until well combined. Stir in the melted chocolate mixture until well combined.
  4. Add the flour and salt to the bowl and stir until the ingredients are well combined and a thick batter forms.
  5. Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan and smooth the top with a spatula. Sprinkle any additional ingredients (such as chocolate chips or nuts) over the top of the batter, if desired.
  6. Place the pan in the oven and bake for 30-35 minutes, or until the brownies are set and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. Remove the pan from the oven and let the brownies cool completely in the pan. Once cool, use the parchment paper to lift the brownies out of the pan and cut them into squares.
  8. Serve the brownies as is, or with a dollop of whipped cream or a scoop of vanilla ice cream on top. Enjoy!
